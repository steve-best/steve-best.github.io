Now

(March 2025) These are the things that I am doing, watching, reading, listening to, and thinking about in no particular order.

Doing

I'm still studying for my CISM certification.

My middle kid is about to turn 20 and is a junior in college (slated to graduate in December 2025). 2005 does not seem like it was so long ago. I’m excited for what the future has in store.

My oldest has been accepted into law school at the University of South Carolina and is slated to graduate college in May. I’m so proud of him.

My daughter is in two theater productions at the moment, and I’m very excited to finally see these plays that she has been putting in so many hours of hard work on.

Watching

I’m watching a lot of NBA basketball and as many Golden State Warriors games as I can. My family and I eagerly await each new episode of The Pitt. If you have not seen this show yet, you’re really missing out. It’s hands down the best show on TV right now. It’s intense though.

Ecstasy of Order: The Tetris Masters is currently streaming on Tubi. Having grown up playing Tetris (and as someone who still plays Tetris just about every day), this movie is phenomenal.

Reading

I’m still reading Jurassic Park on Google Play Books.

Listening

I’ve been listening to a lot of 80’s hair bands and New Wave music as of late, and I have been streaming a lot of First Wave and Lithium on Sirius XM. I’ve been listening to a lot of Jazz while I work.

Thinking About

I’ve been thinking a lot about vendor/platform lock in, and I have been contemplating whether or not it is finally time to give in and get a new phone. I’m toying with the idea of getting a Google Pixel, but I will probably end up with an iPhone so long as Verizon has a good deal on one when the time comes. My wife really wants me to stay on an iPhone, and that has a lot of sway in my decision. Ideally, I think using Apple’s hardware with Google’s apps (i.e. Chrome, GMail, Google Calendar, Google Tasks, and Google Keep) as my defaults works best for me. I am not a Mac user, and I don’t like the idea of vendor lock-in to Apple’s ecosystem, since their ecosystem is not truly cross platform. (Apple treats the web like a second class citizen in my opinion.) I would ideally like an iPhone Plus or Pro Max, since I want to have the biggest screen I can get.
